Our Story

Discover the legacy behind Elite Motors and our commitment to automotive excellence.

Redefining the Luxury Car Buying Experience

Founded in 1995, Elite Motors has established itself as the premier destination for discerning automotive enthusiasts seeking the finest luxury and performance vehicles. From our state-of-the-art showroom to our personalized concierge service, every aspect of the Elite Motors experience is designed to exceed expectations.

Our passion for automobiles goes beyond mere transportation—we celebrate the artistry, engineering, and innovation that define the world's most prestigious automotive brands. With over 25 years of experience, our team has cultivated relationships with manufacturers and collectors worldwide, allowing us to source exceptional vehicles that cannot be found elsewhere.

Whether you're acquiring your first luxury vehicle or expanding your collection, Elite Motors provides an unparalleled level of service, expertise, and access to the most exclusive automobiles available.

Our Journey

From humble beginnings to industry leader, discover the milestones that define Elite Motors.

1995 - Founding Year

Johnathan and Elizabeth Windsor establish Elite Motors in a small downtown location, specializing in European luxury vehicles.

2002 - First Expansion

Relocated to our current flagship facility with double the showroom space and expanded service center capabilities.

2010 - Certified Program Launch

Introduced our Elite Certified Pre-Owned program, setting new industry standards for pre-owned luxury vehicle certification.

2015 - Collector Division

Established our Collector Car Division, specializing in rare and limited-production vehicles for discerning enthusiasts.

2020 - Digital Showroom

Launched our virtual showroom experience, allowing clients worldwide to explore our inventory remotely with 360° views and live video walkarounds.
