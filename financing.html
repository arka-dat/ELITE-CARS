Frequently Asked Questions

What credit score do I need to qualify for financing?

We work with a wide range of credit profiles. While preferred rates typically require a score of 700 or higher, we have financing options available for scores as low as 580. Our financial specialists can help find the best solution for your situation.

What documents do I need to apply for financing?

Typically, you'll need:

  • Valid driver's license
  • Proof of income (recent pay stubs or tax returns)
  • Proof of residence (utility bill or lease agreement)
  • Insurance information
  • References (for certain financing programs)

How long does the financing approval process take?

Most applications receive a preliminary decision within 1-2 business hours during normal business hours. Complete funding typically takes 24-48 hours once all documentation is received. We offer expedited processing for qualified clients.
